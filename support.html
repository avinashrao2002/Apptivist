FAQs

Who can see your profile? Everyone has access to viewing your profile, but if there are specific users you would like to prehibit from viewing your profile, you can issue a complaint through the website's contact page.

I can't login; is my account disabled? If you can't login, the only issue could be that you are entering the wrong email and password into the login. Firebase authentication handles all our login credentials so there's no way we can view your information, but we can manually disable your account if you don't comply with our community guidelines.
